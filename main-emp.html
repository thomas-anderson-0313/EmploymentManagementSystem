New Employee Registration:
the Employee is new to the organization then he/she has to register in the new Employee registration form.

Update Employee:
If the Employee wants to update his profile, he has to update the update employee form.

Apply Leave:
If the Employee wants to apply for a Leave, he will apply in the Apply leave form. It contains the leave date, apply date, no of days, and reason fields.

View Salary Report:
If the Employee wants to see his salary report then he can see it in view salary form .

Check Leave:
In check Leave Employee can see the status of the applied leave.

Work Sheet:
In worksheet Employee can enter the work of the day.
